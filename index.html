Linguistic Pursuits

Scriptmaking

I have been working on making alternate alphabets for english, as well as cyphers for encoding data since a very young age. These used to only use the english alphabet and numbers, but have recently spiraled into more complex situations. I have begun to incorporate my own sounds, symbols, and subsystems within it to create a rich and complex writing system for a language I plan to make.

I currently have three languages that I am trying to make, one of them being a sign language for deaf speakers, one of them involving a very rigid structure for word formation and for speech, and one being based off of ideas and formed casually.
